About Me

Hello, I'm Himanshu Prajapati

Graphic Designer & UI/UX Designer

I love making things look great and work smoothly. For me, design is not just about visuals — it’s about creating experiences that are simple and enjoyable, whether it’s a website, social media post, or a brand identity.

I’m always learning and pushing myself to get better at design. My goal is to bring fresh ideas and make every project more useful and easy to enjoy.

I first got interested in design because I was curious about how pictures and layouts can change how people feel and make decisions. Since then, I’ve learned by taking online classes, practicing a lot, and doing real projects for friends or just for fun.
