Velora – Cosmetic Brand Identity

LuxeThreads is a modern fashion label that blends luxury with sustainability. This project involved creating a complete brand identity system — including logo design, packaging, product tags, social media visuals, and product mockups — to reflect a refined, timeless image

Client

Personal Branding Project

Role

Logo Design, Visual Identity Packaging, Social Media Design, Print Collateral

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Concept

LuxeThreads blends modern design with timeless elegance, creating sustainable, high-quality clothing. Our goal is to inspire confidence with versatile styles that are luxurious, stylish, and eco-friendly.

The Velora logo uses a graceful serif typeface to reflect beauty, elegance, and sophistication. The custom “V” adds a unique, signature-like feel, while the lowercase “cosmetic” balances the design with softness and approachability.

A minimal “V” monogram that captures Velora’s elegance in a compact, timeless form.

Print Design

Velora's product design is all about clean elegance. Each item—from the cleanser to the serum—was styled to look luxurious yet soft, using muted tones and refined typography. I kept the packaging minimal so the focus stays on purity and self-care.
