LuxeThreads - Fashion Brand Identity

LuxeThreads is a modern, upscale fashion brand that seamlessly blends contemporary design with timeless elegance. We craft high-quality, sustainable clothing for discerning, style-conscious individuals. Our collections are inspired by the intersection of art, culture, and fashion, capturing the dynamic essence of modern style.

Personal Branding Project

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Concept

LuxeThreads blends modern design with timeless elegance, creating sustainable, high-quality clothing. Our goal is to inspire confidence with versatile styles that are luxurious, stylish, and eco-friendly.

The LuxeThreads logo features a sleek “LT” monogram inside a circle, symbolising elegance and unity. Its minimal style reflects the brand’s luxury and modern aesthetic.Designed to work across tags, packaging, and digital platforms.

I created logo variations to ensure flexibility across different brand touchpoints. The full wordmark is ideal for packaging and web, while the circular “LT” monogram works well on small spaces like tags, social icons, and labels.

The icon is a simple, circular “LT” monogram that represents the initials of LuxeThreads. I designed it to be bold, minimal, and easy to recognize — perfect for small formats like tags, buttons, stickers, and social media.

Social Media & Banner

I designed social media posts and banners that match the brand’s clean, elegant style. Using soft tones, minimal layouts, and stylish typography, each design aims to communicate luxury while staying visually consistent across platforms.

Packaging

Designed to delight at first glance—this packaging blends rich coffee browns, soft creams, and a touch of green to evoke warmth, freshness, and the comforting ritual of premium coffee, delivered

Print Design

This is where the brand comes to life in a physical form. I designed every printed piece from coffee packaging and takeaway cups to business cards and labels to feel consistent with Café Crate’s cozy and modern vibe. I focused on keeping the layout clean, the type easy to read, and the colors warm and inviting. My goal was to make sure every item, whether it’s picked up in a shop or handed to a customer, gives off the same comforting and premium feel as the café itself. It’s more than just good design. it’s about building a full experience, even in print.
