Café Crate – Brand Identity Design

Café Crate is a coffee brand that offers freshly brewed coffee, delicious donuts, and baked treats. It’s a place where people can enjoy high-quality drinks and food in a warm and cozy setting. Café Crate focuses on using the best ingredients to create a great experience for every customer, whether they’re grabbing coffee on the go or relaxing with friends.

Client

Personal Branding Project

Role

Logo Design, Visual Identity Packaging, Social Media Design, Print Collateral

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Concept

The Concept of Café Crate is to make every coffee moment special. We aim to serve the best coffee and baked goods while creating a space where people can feel comfortable and connected. Our focus is on quality, taste, and making every visit memorable.

A minimalist logo designed for a premium coffee delivery brand. Featuring a stylized cup within a crate, the mark reflects warmth, accessibility, and modern aesthetics conveying a sense of comfort, community, and thoughtful design

A clean and modern logo combining a coffee cup and crate to represent warmth, convenience, and café culture in one simple mark.

Transforming vision into iconic symbols. Our logos speak bold, clear, and creative identity that lasts in every mind.

Packaging

Designed to delight at first glance—this packaging blends rich coffee browns, soft creams, and a touch of green to evoke warmth, freshness, and the comforting ritual of premium coffee, delivered with care.

Social Media & Poster

For the social media part of this project, I wanted every post to feel like an extension of the brand warm, inviting, and visually consistent.
I used clean layouts, natural colors, and real café moments to create a feed that not only promotes products but also connects with the audience emotionally. Whether it's a quote, a product highlight, or a cozy in-café moment, every design supports the brand story.
The goal was to make the feed feel like you’re scrolling through the atmosphere of Café Crate calm, personal, and crafted with care

Print Design

This is where the brand comes to life in a physical form. I designed every printed piece from coffee packaging and takeaway cups to business cards and labels to feel consistent with Café Crate's cozy and modern vibe.

I focused on keeping the layout clean, the type easy to read, and the colors warm and inviting. My goal was to make sure every item, whether it's picked up in a shop or handed to a customer, gives off the same comforting and premium feel as the café itself. It's more than just good design. it's about building a full experience, even in print.
